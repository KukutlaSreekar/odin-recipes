Sambar

Description

Sambar is a lentil-based vegetable stew, cooked with
pigeon pea and tamarind broth. It originates in South Indian cuisine 
and is popular in large parts of India and beyond.

Ingredients

  • Toor dal - pigeon peas
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Sambar powder
  • Vegetables - carrot, potato, drumstick, etc.
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry red chilies

Steps:

  1. Cook 1 cup of Toor dal until soft.
  2. Soak and extract tamarind pulp.
  3. Chop vegetables (carrot, potato, drumstick).
  4. Cook dal, tamarind, and veggies together.
  5. Add sambar powder, salt, and spices.
  6. Prepare tempering with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dry chilies.
  7. Pour tempering over sambar and mix.
  8. Serve hot with rice or dosa.
